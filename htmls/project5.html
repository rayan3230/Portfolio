Project 5: Vertex - University Student Resources Platform

Project Description

Vertex is a web platform designed for university students to access and share study resources, documents, and exam materials. The platform aims to make it easier for students to find and contribute helpful academic content, supporting collaborative learning and exam preparation.

Key Features:

  • Browse and search for study resources and documents by subject or course
  • Upload and share your own notes, past exams, and study guides
  • Organized resource library for easy access
  • User-friendly interface for students

Technologies Used:

  • JavaScript
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
