Project 6: Serie Tracker

Project Description

Serie Tracker is your personal companion for discovering, tracking, and managing your favorite TV series. Whether you're a binge-watcher or a casual viewer, our platform helps you keep track of what you've watched, explore new releases, and organize your watchlist with ease.

Key Features:

  • Track your watched and unwatched series
  • Discover trending and new releases
  • Maintain a wishlist for future viewing
  • View detailed information and posters for each show
  • Enjoy a modern, user-friendly interface

Technologies Used:

  • React
  • Django
  • JavaScript
