About Serie Tracker
Serie Tracker is your personal companion for discovering, tracking, and managing your favorite TV series. Whether you're a binge-watcher or a casual viewer, our platform helps you keep track of what you've watched, explore new releases, and organize your watchlist with ease.

✔️ Track your watched and unwatched series
✔️ Discover trending and new releases
✔️ Maintain a wishlist for future viewing
✔️ View detailed information and posters for each show
✔️ Enjoy a modern, user-friendly interface
Built with React and Django, Serie Tracker is designed to make your TV experience organized and enjoyable. Happy watching!
